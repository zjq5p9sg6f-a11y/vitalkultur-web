2. Principle: No Data Collection

CARLON does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to servers, third parties, or the developer. The app works entirely offline.

3. Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Session history: Date, duration, preset name, retention times.
  • Settings: Language, haptic intensity, sound configuration, color theme.
  • Custom presets: Breathing presets you create.
  • Coach profile (optional): Name, tagline, profile photo — local only.

This data never leaves your device.

4. Apple HealthKit

With your explicit consent, CARLON uses Apple HealthKit:

  • Write: Mindful minutes saved to Apple Health.
  • Read: Heart rate, HRV (SDNN), and resting heart rate for post-session insights.

HealthKit data is processed locally only. It is never transmitted to servers, never used for advertising, and never shared with third parties.

5. No Analytics, No Tracking, No Ads

CARLON uses no analytics tools, no tracking pixels, no ad SDKs, no user accounts, no server-side processing, and no cookies.

6. In-App Purchases

Payments are processed exclusively by Apple via StoreKit. The developer has no access to payment data.

7. Watch-iPhone Sync

Data syncs between your Apple Watch and iPhone via Apple's WatchConnectivity framework — device-to-device, no servers involved.

8. Children

CARLON is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
