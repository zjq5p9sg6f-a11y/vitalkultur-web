How it started

An app that began as
a private experiment.

CARLON was never meant to be a product. It started as a personal experiment — because we couldn't find a breathing app that truly convinced us. It was born during the most intense time of our lives: when our son came into the world. Our midwife showed us dozens of breathing techniques for the birth. We were fascinated. We wanted them on our Watch. Eyes closed, just feel.

The experiment became a passion project. Friends tried the app and said: You need to share this. So here we are.

The name? Our son is called Carlon — the free, beloved man. Carl means "the free one", and we see a freedom particle in every syllable. A name for a person who taught us how to breathe again.

Close your eyes

Your wrist guides you. Inhale feels like rising, exhale like descending. You don't need to look — just feel. That's how we imagined it.

Foundation

Science,
not guesswork.

Each of our 16 techniques has a scientific foundation. We don't promise miracles — we give you tools that work.

Stanford · Cell Reports Medicine · 2023

Cyclic sighing improves mood more than meditation. RCT, n=108.

PNAS · Kox et al. · 2014

Tummo protocol and immune markers. RCT, n=24. The experiment that brought breathwork into science.

Frontiers · Lehrer & Gevirtz · 2014

Coherence breathing at 5.5/min. Heart, breath and blood pressure synchronize. Peak HRV.

Physiological Reports · Vierra et al. · 2022

4-7-8 breathing technique by Dr. Andrew Weil. Effects on heart rate and autonomic balance. n=43.

Behind the scenes

Built with
obsession.

When Apple introduced the new haptic capabilities of the Watch, we knew: this has to feel like breathing. We spent months deep in the haptic engine — every inhale impulse rises, every exhale descends. The double-tap gesture on Apple Watch Ultra ends a breath hold without looking. Everything intentional, everything felt.

We deliberately built in many ways to customize — your own presets, color themes, soundscapes, haptic levels. Maybe not everything is perfect yet. But we're working continuously to make every detail better. We're especially curious about your haptic feedback — tell us how it feels.

And this is just the beginning. New techniques, deeper analytics, coach marketplace, things we can't reveal yet. The quality of the app shows you how serious we are.
