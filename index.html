Setup for the best experience

🔇 Set your Watch to silent mode

Side button → tap the bell icon. You'll feel every haptic pulse guiding your breath — but hear only the singing bowl and instruments, no system beeps. This is the way CARLON is designed to be used.

⌚ Enable Always-On Display

Settings → Display & Brightness → Always On. This keeps the Flower of Life breathing animation visible when your wrist is down, and enables Double Tap gesture recognition.

✌️ How Double Tap works

During breath holds, gently turn your wrist to wake the display, then tap your thumb and index finger together twice. This ends the hold phase — no need to look at or touch the watch. Requires Apple Watch Series 9 or Ultra 2.

📱 iPhone Companion App

The iPhone app syncs automatically with your Watch. View session history, HRV trends, retention progress, and share your stats with friends. Create custom presets on either device.

🎵 Sound Settings

CARLON uses a real singing bowl recorded by hand. You can enable/disable each sound layer independently: bowl, breath texture, phase markers, and an optional Schumann resonance (7.83 Hz) ambient drone for deep meditation.

🤖 Siri & Shortcuts

Say "Start breathing in CARLON" or "Show my breathwork stats". Export your data as JSON for AI analysis with ChatGPT or Claude. 7 shortcuts built in — all work with Siri, Shortcuts app, and automations.
