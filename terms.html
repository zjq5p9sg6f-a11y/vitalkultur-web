4. Safety Instructions

When using the app, the following safety rules apply:

  • Never practice advanced breath retention techniques while standing, in water, or while operating vehicles or machinery
  • Always practice seated or lying on a stable surface
  • Stop immediately if you experience discomfort, chest pain, or visual disturbances
  • Tingling and mild dizziness are normal responses to controlled hyperventilation techniques
  • The Provider strongly recommends enabling Fall Detection (Settings → Emergency SOS → Fall Detection) and setting up a Medical ID on your Apple Watch
